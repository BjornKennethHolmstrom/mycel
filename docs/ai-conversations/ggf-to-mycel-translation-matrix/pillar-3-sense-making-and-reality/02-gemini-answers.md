### 3. Handling Conflicting Verifications
* **The Question:** *How to handle conflicting verifications?*
* **The Vulnerability:** DeepSeek suggests "Mycel shows both. Users choose". Simply dropping two conflicting facts in a thread replicates the chaotic, toxic "reply guy" dynamics of legacy social media.
